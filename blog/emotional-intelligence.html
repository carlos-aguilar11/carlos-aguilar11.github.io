What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to perceive, manage and use your emotions.

How is EQ different to IQ?

Emotional Quotient (EQ) measures a person’s emotional competencies and their ability to recognise their own and other people's emotional expression. While Intelligence Quotient (IQ) measures a person’s cognitive and reasoning abilities.

Why is EQ important?

Because it helps you to connect with other people, communicate effectively, resolve problems and express yourself with empathy.
